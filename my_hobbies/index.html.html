Planting

I have a passion for planting and taking care of various plants. Whether it's growing flowers, herbs, or vegetables, I find it incredibly rewarding to nurture something from a seed and watch it grow into a full, thriving plant.

My favorite plants to grow are sunflowers and lavender because they bring so much color and fragrance to the garden.

Pottery

I also enjoy pottery. Working with clay allows me to express my creativity in a hands-on way. I enjoy shaping clay into bowls, vases, or decorative pieces.

Each piece is unique, and the process of turning raw clay into something functional is incredibly fulfilling.
